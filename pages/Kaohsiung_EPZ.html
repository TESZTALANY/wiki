Kaohsiung Export Processing Zone

The Kaohsiung Export Processing Zone was the only export processing zome in Asia in 1966 when it opened.

The EPZ was a breach in Taiwan's protectionsit economy. It was a small district within Kaohsiung, within the bounds of which a set of generous tax incentives would be active that aim to encourage investment.

The export oriented economy allowed Taiwan's GDP to grow by around 10% throughout the 1960s and 70s. Their success was later copied by the PRC's equivalent Special Economic Zones.
