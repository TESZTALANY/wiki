Attempted assassination of Chen Shui-bian

An assassination attempt was carried out against DPP politicians Chen Shui-bian and his running mate Anette Lu the night before the 2004 presidential election.

Chen and Lu were touring the streets of Tainan in an open jeep, when they noticed that they had been shot in the chest and the knee respectively. Both of them recovered, but the shooter has not been identified since.
