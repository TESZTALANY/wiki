People's Republic of China

The People's Republic of China (PRC, Chinese: 中華人民共和國; Pinyin: Zhōnghuá Rénmín Gònghéguó) is a country officially established on the 1st of October, 1949 by Communist leader Mao Zedong. It maintains jurisdiction over Mainland China and is recognised by the UN and most countries as the one and only China.
