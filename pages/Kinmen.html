Kinmen islands mere kilometers from China

Kinmen

The Kinmen archipelago is a set of small islands under Taiwanese jurisdiction, despite being located only a few kilometers off the coast of the Fujianese port city of Xiamen.

It was used by the ROC during the late stages of the Chinese Civil War to broadcast propaganda to the Communist-controlled Mainland. Heavy fortifications helped it maintain control over it to this day. (as of writing)
