Annette Lu

Annette Lu (Chinese: 呂秀蓮; Pinyin: Lǚ Xiùlián) is a feminist activist and DPP politician. She was initially hoisted into the limelight by an article of hers written in 1971 in which she criticised plans to restrict the number of women allowed to work at universities. She later ran for and won the post of vice-president in the 2000 and 2004 presidential elections.
