Chen Yi

Chen Yi was the provincial governor of Taiwan Province in the early years of ROC occupation. His reign was characterised by economic turmoil as his administration seized 90% of the island's economy, causing a subsequent. Corruption became rampant and illnesses such as malaria and the bubonic plague reappeared on the island.

Chen is also credited with the resolution of the infamous 2-28 Incident being as violent as it was.
